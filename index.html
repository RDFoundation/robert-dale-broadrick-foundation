Robert Dale Broadrick Memorial Scholarship

Honoring Service, Leadership, and Community

In Loving Memory of Robert Dale Broadrick

The Robert Dale Broadrick Memorial Scholarship has been established by family and friends to honor the memory and commitment of Robert Dale to the community, family, military, outdoors, and the students of East Robertson High School.

Robert Dale was a rising Junior at East Robertson High School and a cadet in the Air Force Junior ROTC. Known for his hardworking nature and good work ethic, Robert Dale had a great sense of humor, enjoyed good clean fun, and always greeted people with a hug and a smile.

His family and friends were his world and he took pride in everything he did. Robert Dale had such a tremendous impact on the East Robertson and Robertson County community and will be truly missed.

About the Scholarship

This scholarship is funded by the Broadrick family to honor Robert Dale's legacy and support students in the Robertson County community who share his dedication to service, education, and personal excellence.

Award Amount

Four (4) scholarships worth $5,000 each will be awarded annually.

Eligibility

Graduating seniors at East Robertson High School or members of the Junior ROTC of Robertson County, Tennessee.

Application Deadline

May 15, 2026

For the 2026-2027 academic year

Eligibility Criteria

  • Graduating High School Senior from East Robertson High School or member of Junior ROTC of Robertson County, TN
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Planning to attend a community college, university, or technical school

Selection Process

Scholarship recipients will be selected by the Broadrick family. Four (4) scholarships worth $5,000 each will be awarded annually. The family's decision shall be final. The Robert Dale Broadrick Foundation, Inc., a tax-exempt charitable foundation, will administer the Robert Dale Broadrick Memorial Scholarship.

Payment of Awards

Payments will be made at the beginning of the school year or at the beginning of each semester. Checks will be made payable to the school for the student's account and will be forwarded to the financial aid office.
